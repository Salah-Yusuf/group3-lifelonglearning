Hello, I’m Hazyq Norashraf

I’m an 18-year-old student currently pursuing a Foundation in Computing at MMU. You might be wondering—why MMU? Why choose FCI? My answer is simple: Why not? I’ve been passionate about IT since high school, where I first discovered my interest through Computer Science in Form 4. That early spark grew into a genuine curiosity about coding, problem-solving, and how technology shapes the world around us. As for MMU, I chose it because of its strong reputation in the industry. Many professionals I’ve met often mention that MMU students are highly regarded and prioritized when it comes to opportunities in the tech field. That gave me the confidence to believe this is the right place to start my journey.
